Shipping Policy

Processing Time

Orders are typically processed and shipped within 1-3 business days after payment confirmation, excluding weekends and holidays. Orders with customized or made-to-order items may require additional processing time. Please refer to the product description for specific details.

Shipping Methods

We offer standard shipping options, including Canada Post, with estimated delivery times of 5-10 business days. Expedited shipping options are available for an additional fee. Please select your preferred shipping method during checkout.

Shipping Rates

Shipping rates are calculated based on the weight and destination of your order. You can view the shipping cost at checkout before completing your purchase. We may offer free shipping promotions for orders that meet certain criteria. Please check our website or promotional emails for details.

Order Tracking

Once your order has shipped, you will receive a shipping confirmation email with a tracking number. You can use this tracking number to monitor the status of your shipment online. Please note that tracking information may take up to 24 hours to update after shipment.

International Shipping

We currently offer international shipping to select countries. International shipping rates and delivery times may vary based on destination and customs clearance processes. Customers are responsible for any customs duties, taxes, or import fees incurred upon delivery.

Shipping Restrictions

We comply with all local, state, and federal regulations regarding shipping restrictions for certain products. Please review our product listings for any applicable restrictions.

Shipping Address

Please ensure that your shipping address is accurate and complete to avoid delays or misdelivery of your order. We are not responsible for orders shipped to incorrect or incomplete addresses provided by the customer.

Lost or Damaged Shipments

In the event that your shipment is lost or damaged during transit, please contact us immediately. We will work with the carrier to resolve the issue and ensure that you receive a satisfactory resolution.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about our shipping policy, or if you need assistance with your order, please contact us at information@lilidan.online.
